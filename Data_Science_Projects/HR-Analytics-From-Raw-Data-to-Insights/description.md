### **HR Analytics Dashboard: From Raw Data to Business Insights**

#### **Project Overview**

This project demonstrates how to transform raw messy employee data into actionable business insights using Python. It focuses on analyzing employee performance, salary trends, regional distributions, and departmental metrics to support data-driven decision-making for HR teams.

#### **Objective**

To develop an end-to-end HR analytics pipeline from data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) to visualization providing a clear overview of workforce patterns and performance indicators.

#### **Key Features**

- **ETL Pipeline:** Reads, cleans, and prepares HR datasets for analysis.\\  
- **Business Insights Generation:**  
  - Annual hiring trends.\\  
  - Department-wise performance and salary analysis.\\  
  - Regional employee distribution.\\  
  - Salary comparison by remote work and performance.\\  
- **Visualization Dashboard:**  
  Professionally designed plots (bar, pie, and distribution charts) summarizing key metrics in a single visual framework.

#### **Tech Stack**

- **Programming Language:** Python\\  
- **Libraries:** pandas, matplotlib, numpy\\  
- **Environment:** Jupyter Notebook

#### **Outcomes**

The cleaned data , final dashboard and analytical outputs help HR professionals:\\

- Identify hiring and retention patterns.\\  
- Evaluate departmental performance.\\  
- Compare compensation and productivity across locations and job types.
